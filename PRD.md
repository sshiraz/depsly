# DepSly PRD

## Product Summary
Dependency Intelligence Platform for npm.

## Core Value
Understand and monitor dependency risk before and after install.

## Features

### Pre-adoption
- package lookup
- risk scoring

### Post-adoption (future)
- monitoring
- alerts

## Risk Model
- blast radius
- maintainer risk
- activity risk
- CVE placeholder

## Actions
- Replace
- Review
- Monitor
- Accept

## Goal
Provide clear decisions, not just analysis.
